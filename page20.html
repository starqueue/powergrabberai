Section II

Additional Regulatory Frameworks

The same visibility that supports EU AI Act compliance directly addresses requirements in GDPR, SOC 2, and ISO 27001.

GDPR · Article 30

Records of Processing Activities

Article 30 requires controllers to maintain records of all processing activities. When AI tools process personal data, PowerGrabber logs which provider received the data, when, and from which application — creating the processing record automatically.

SOC 2 · CC6 / CC7

Logical Access & System Operations

SOC 2 requires monitoring of system components and detection of anomalous activity. PowerGrabber's per-tool API key tracking and rate-limit alerting satisfy logical access monitoring and change detection controls for AI systems.

ISO 27001 · A.8

Asset Management & Access Control

ISO 27001 Annex A.8 requires an inventory of assets and control of access. PowerGrabber's automatic tool and provider discovery creates a continuously-updated asset inventory for all AI systems, with policy-based access controls.

The EU AI Act enters into force in stages. Organisations should begin building their AI visibility foundation now.

The visibility prerequisite

Every obligation in the EU AI Act — risk classification, transparency, human oversight, record-keeping — presupposes that you know which AI systems your organisation is using. Without an automated discovery mechanism, compliance is built on incomplete data.

Aug 2025

Prohibited AI practices take effect

Organisations must have identified any AI systems that fall under prohibited categories. PowerGrabber's provider inventory ensures no AI system is overlooked.

Aug 2026

Obligations for high-risk AI systems apply

Risk management, data governance, transparency, and human oversight obligations become enforceable. PowerGrabber provides the data foundation for all four.

Aug 2027

Full enforcement including penalties

Non-compliance penalties up to 35 million EUR or 7% of global annual turnover. Complete AI system visibility is not optional at this stage.

HIPAA Implications

When Protected Health Information (PHI) reaches a system without a Business Associate Agreement, the consequences are immediate and severe.

⚠

PHI to Non-BAA System

OpenAI's standard API does not include a Business Associate Agreement. Any PHI transmitted to this endpoint constitutes an unauthorized disclosure under HIPAA.

⚖

Penalty Structure

HIPAA violations for unauthorized PHI disclosure range from $100 to $50,000 per violation, with an annual maximum of $2,078,640 per violation category.

📋

Breach Notification Required

If PHI was included in these requests, your organisation must notify affected individuals, HHS, and potentially the media within 60 days of discovery.

🔒

Willful Neglect Risk

If your organisation knew AI tools were in use but had no monitoring in place, regulators may classify this as "willful neglect" — the highest penalty tier.
